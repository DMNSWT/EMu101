Modifying records

1. Open the record you wish to modify
In our example we are logged in as Earth Sciences 4 and we are viewing a record in the Catalog module.
2. Modify the fields as necessary
Here I have added a row to the "Other Numbers" table in the "Catalog (Geol)" tab.
3. Click the file disk icon in the toolbar to save the modification

Alternatively, hit CTRL+S in the keyboard to save changes (or go to File, then Save Record in the top menu).

4. Click the "forbidden" icon to cancel and discard changes
