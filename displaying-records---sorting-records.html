Displaying records - sorting records

1. You can sort records by any field available in the module you are in

We are logged in as an Education Collections Manager. We are viewing the results of a search in the Localities module, where the Locality Number field could be anything (except for empty). The current View chosen displays the Locality Number, State, County and Verbatim Locality fields.

2. Click the sort icon in the toolbar (AZ with an arrow icon)
3. A pop-up window will appear

Just like a list View, we have to create Sort options so we can select what field(s) we want to sort by and how. Click "New".

4. Name your Sort something relevant

Since we want to sort our localities by the Locality Number field, let's add the name "LocalityNumber" and select the appropriate field and click add to use it as a sorting option. Click "OK" when done.

5. Click the newly created "LocalityNumber" Sort option
6. Click "OK"
7. Before your records are sorted, a dialog will appear

Click "No" to ignore the summary of the sort terms. Depending on the number of records and fields your are sorting by, EMu might take up to a few minutes to provide you with results.

8. Your records should be displayed in a sorted list

By default, the sorting process is done alphabetically or numerically (lower to higher), depending on the field(s) used. If you want to invert the sorting order, do it so in the Sort properties.
